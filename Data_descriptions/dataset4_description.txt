Information about dataset:

This dataset contains full historical daily price and volume data for all US-based stocks in Karachi stock.
This Data Contain 801 companies that are registered in Karachi Stock Market, Pakistan.

Information about attributes:

1 - Symbol
2 - Date
3 - Open
4 - High
5 - Low
6 - Close
7 - Volume
